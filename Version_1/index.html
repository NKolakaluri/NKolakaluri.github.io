Dallas Texas
Visited: Aug 8, 2016 - Aug 15, 2016
Dallas always has a place in me due to the memories I made as a child growing up there. Although the main memory I have is how painful the scorching heat was. Anyways, my family and I decided to go visit some of our closest childhood friends in Seattle who had to move to Texas. After seeing them for the first time again in a few years it felt as if they never left. During this trip we ate loads of food and I experienced my very first outdoor nature pool. This pool was definetely the highlight of the trip(besides reuniting with my friends) due to how unique of a pool it was. The freezing cold water, slimy rocks beneath my feet, vines on the side walls with a stunning waterfall made it seem like I was one with nature.
