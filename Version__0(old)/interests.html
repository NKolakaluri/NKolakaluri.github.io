Piano
I Guess I'm Pretty Classic
Though it may seem as if I were naturally drawn towards music, there was one instrument that initially sparked my interest, the piano. At 5 years of age, I started playing the piano and had a lot of fun playing nursery rhymes such as twinkle twinkle little star. However, after a year and a half of playing for hours a day I just found myself progressing too slowly and had to drop it. But that wasn't the end of it, during quarantine I found myself with a lot of extra time, so I decided to pick it up again. With motivation from a friend who listens to classical music literally all day, I decided to learn some classical pieces and found it to be very enjoyable.
